The project

The Vision: A series of high-yield, low-supply cryptocoins, specifically and intentionally targeted towards the avid CryptoCoin collector. Those who collect them not only for the purpose of having them grow in value, but also as a stable store of this value over time.

The Mission: The CryptoCollectibles team have put together a plan to launch a bold new series of CryptoCoins (note the absence of the word "Currency"?), for the sole purpose of providing the keen CryptoCoin follower a means of trading and collecting CryptoCoins which are designed with high-yield and relatively low supply.

This is a paradigm shift in the Crypto space - the Cryptocollectible coins are NOT designed to be currency. We will not claim now or later that they were designed to be exchanged for goods and services. Instead, our coins are designed to be collected, and of course traded, providing collectors a stable, high-yielding store of value over time. Did you ever (or do you currently) collect stamps, silver/gold coins, comic-books etc? If so, then you have an idea what our coins seek to be - the coin of choice for the keen Crypto Fan. Our coins will grow in value over time, as they are traded and collected.

Pulsar coin

PULSAR is the first in the Cryptocollectibles series of themed collectors' CryptoCoins.

INTRODUCING PULSAR - THE FIRST IN THE CRYPTOCOLLECTIBLES THEMED COINS

Quote pul·sar /'p?l?sär/ noun ASTRONOMY
A celestial object, thought to be a rapidly rotating neutron star, that emits regular pulses of radio waves and other electromagnetic radiation at rates of up to one thousand pulses per second.

  • Name: Pulsar
  • Symbol: PLSR
  • Consensus: PoW + PoS
  • PoW Total Blocks: 150000
  • PoW Payout: Pulsating 1 and 10 PLSR per block
  • Confirmations for blocks to mature: 10
  • Algorithm: HMQ1725 (CPU/GPU Only)
  • PoS Payout: Pulsating 1 and 10 PLSR per block
  • Confirmations required for staking: 50
  • Block target (seconds): 60
  • Retarget difficulty: DGW
  • Max supply: 15000000
  • Premine: 150000
